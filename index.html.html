Dyslexia

Dysliexa, also known as reading disorder, is charactized by trouble reading despite normal intelligence. Different people are affected to varying degress. Problems may include difficulties in spelling words, reading quickly, writing words, sounding out words in the head, pronoucning words when reading aloud and understanding what one reads.

Often these difficultes are first noticed at school.

ADHD

ADHD (Attention Defict Hyperactivity Disorder) is a mental disorder of the neruodevelopmental type. It is characteirzed by diffculty paying attenion, execssive activity and acting without regards to consequences, which are otherwise not apporpiate for a person's age. Some people with ADHD also display difficulty regulating emotions. For a diagnosis, the symptoms should appear before a person is 12 years old, be present for more than six months and cause problems in at least 2 settings.
